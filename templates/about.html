Energuru

Energuru is an interactive web platform with a sophisticated predictive model designed to assess the energy consumption of residential dwelling units, contingent upon their distinctive attributes and geographical location. The principal aim of Energuru centers on furnishing valuable assistance to homeowners, prospective buyers, and real estate professionals by furnishing estimations of the prospective energy consumption of a dwelling unit, while also delivering customized recommendations to ameliorate energy utilization. In doing so, Energuru seeks to foster transparency and impart data-driven insights that can contribute to informed negotiations, guide prudent investment choices, and cultivate a property market that embraces sustainability and energy consciousness.
